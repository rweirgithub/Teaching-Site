Who am I?

Hi, I'm Ross, an English teacher from Norfolk, England. I've been teaching English for 7 years, and I absolutely love learning and teaching languages. Besides English, I speak French and a little bit of Spanish. I'm passionate about helping people improve their English skills, and I believe that learning a language opens up new opportunities to connect with different cultures and make friends from all over the world. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced learner, I'll be here to guide you through the difficulties of the English language. Grow and gain confidence in your English skills! I can't wait for our first class together, see you then

Frequently asked questions

Classes are as frequent as you like, it all depends on both your and my availability. I generally recommend two classes a week, one at the start and one at the end (Monday and Friday for example). This ensures the student has enough time to take in the information from the first class without it being overwritten in the second.

Please see the next FAQ question on cancellations for more details.

Cancellations are acceptable with 72 hours' notice. This is to gaurentee that I can refund you and still refill that slot with another student. I also allow up to three last minute emergency cancellations in case your child gets sick. If classes are cancelled with less than 72 hours' notice, the credit for that class will be lost.
